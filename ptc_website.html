About PTC

India's Trusted IT Partner for Enterprises

Priyanka Technology Corporation (PTC) is a next-generation IT Consultancy and Technology Services company headquartered in Navi Mumbai, Maharashtra.

Founded by Tushar Barve & Priyanka Barve, PTC delivers end-to-end consultancy for business strategy, digital transformation, and enterprise technology adoption.

As part of the Atul Group — a diversified industrial conglomerate with 500+ clients across India — PTC brings unmatched domain knowledge, industry relationships, and a Pan-India presence from day one.
